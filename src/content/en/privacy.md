# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 10, 2026

## Introduction

The *Faith & Home* Project, conducted by the *Small Cities Lab* at *Lehigh University*, is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all participants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you participate in our survey.

## Information We Collect

When you participate in our survey and interviews, we may collect the following types of information:

### Survey Responses
- Demographic information (age, family composition, geographic location)
- Responses to questions about your relationship to faith and home.

### Technical Information
- IP address (collected automatically for security purposes)
- Browser type and device information
- Survey completion timestamps

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

1. **Research Analysis**: To analyze patterns and trends in how congregations consider their past, present, and future.
2. **Storytelling**: To publish stories connecting faith and home in order to help others understand the issue and imagine futures that work for their congregations and neighbors.
3. **Academic Publication**: To publish aggregate findings in scholarly journals and presentations
4. **Project Improvement**: To improve our research methods and survey instruments
5. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable research regulations and institutional requirements

## Data Protection and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

- All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
- Access to identifying information is restricted to authorized research team members
- Data is stored on secure, password-protected servers

## Data Retention

We will retain your survey responses for the duration of the project and for a period of 2 years following the completion of the study. After this period, all identifying information will be permanently deleted.

## Your Rights

As a participant, you have the right to:

- Withdraw from participation at any time without penalty
- Request access to your survey responses
- Request correction of any inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data (subject to research regulatory requirements)

## Confidentiality

All survey responses are confidential. We will not:

- Share your individual responses with anyone outside the core team
- Publish any information that could identify you personally without explicit permission.
- Use your information for commercial purposes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
